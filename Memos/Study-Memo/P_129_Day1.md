## Simulated trading contest

### Kelly formula

$$
f  = \frac{(b\times p-q)}{b}
$$

Given $b=0.7$, $p=0.6$ , $q=0.4$, $f$ should be $0.02855$. Both team' s trading strategy are inappropriate for investing too much money in each round.
